# CodeBook 

*  subject  -  Number
          Identifier of the subject who carried out the experiment.
                1...30  

*  activity  -  String
          Activities performed by each subject   
               WALKING
               WALKING_UPSTAIRS
               WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
               SITTING
               STANDING
               LAYING
